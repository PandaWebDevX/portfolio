Design ethics

Accessibility.

A great website is designed for all people by default.
That means considering people using assistive technology, and anticipating everyone's ability to process information.

Ethics.

Ethical design values people's safety and privacy first.
It can absolutely support a company's goals if transparency outweighs hidden agendas.

Sustainability.

The web isn't green.
Reducing how much data we store, how many emails we send, and how heavy our pages are serves people's experience as well as the planet.
